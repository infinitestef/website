MYTH Match: The Beautiful Game

This 1v1 card game puts you in charge of a football team of animals, Bench Beasts and Mythic powerhouses. Designed to promote awareness of biomes and biodiversity in the World Cup nations they are based upon, you also get to tell the epic stories of your team's evolution.
